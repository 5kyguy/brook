About Brook

Brook is a fully offline desktop music player. It reads audio from your music library on disk, plays files through a Rust audio engine, and presents a focused desktop interface in Tauri. Nothing here needs the internet.

What you get

  • Library scan and browse with favorites, playlists, and search
  • Playback with queue, shuffle, repeat, and lyrics from sidecar or embedded tags
  • Listening stats, recent plays, and local top-charts playlists
  • Read-only access to your files. Brook does not rewrite tags or re-encode audio
